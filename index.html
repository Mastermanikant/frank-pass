The Stateless Workflow

Three inputs. One mathematical function. One password. No storage.

1

Enter Site / Platform

Type any platform name or paste a URL. FrankPass normalizes it automatically — facebook.com, www.facebook.com, or just facebook all produce the exact same result.

2

Enter Your Secret Key

Your secret key is the seed. It never leaves your device. PBKDF2 with 1,000,000 iterations and HMAC-SHA512 expansion transform it into an unbreakable cryptographic anchor.

3

Copy & Use

Your password is generated in milliseconds — entirely on your device. Copy it, use it, and forget it. The math will reproduce it identically next time. Nothing is ever saved.

The stateless advantage

Why not a cloud password manager?

Cloud vaults can be breached

Every major password manager has had a security incident. When they store your encrypted vault online, they create a honeypot — a single point of failure that makes your entire digital life a target for one attack.

You can't breach what doesn't exist

FrankPass has no servers, no databases, no user accounts, no synced vault. There is simply nothing to steal. Your password exists only during the brief moment it appears on your screen.

Works when the internet doesn't

Cloud managers fail when their servers go down or when you're offline. FrankPass is a PWA — install it once and it works forever, even in airplane mode, even if our domain expired.
