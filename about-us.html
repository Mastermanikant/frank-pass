Origin Story

The night FrankPass was born

In December 2025, Master Manikant Yadav faced a crisis. Managing over 20 Gmail accounts for various YouTube projects, he needed to access a critical years-old email — and had completely forgotten the password. Despite every "forgot password" attempt and recovery flow, he was locked out of his own digital life.

That frustration crystallized a realization: "The world's security is built on a lie. Humans are bad at remembering random strings — but we are exceptional at remembering patterns."

FrankPass was born that night. Not as another vault, not as another app asking for an account — but as a tool built on Mathematical Certainty. You don't save your passwords. You derive them. The same pattern, the same math, the same result — every time, on any device, even offline.

No cloud storage. Your passwords are never written anywhere — not on our servers, not in your browser. They exist only in the moment you generate them.

No fake claims. Every statement on this site is verifiable. The cryptography is open. You can audit frankpass-core.js in your browser's developer tools right now.

Free forever. The web generator will always be free. No paywalls, no dark patterns, no "upgrade to see your password." The free tool does everything you need.
