The Secret Origin: December 2025

FrankPass (originally **IRY PASS**) wasn't born in a high-tech lab. It was born out of sheer frustration one late night in December 2025.

At the time, I was juggling multiple YouTube channel ideas. Every new idea meant a new Gmail ID. I was managing over 20 accounts. That night, I needed to access an old, critical email, but I had completely forgotten the password. No recovery option worked, and I was locked out of my own digital life.

That frustration turned into research. I realized the world doesn't need "vaults" that can be hacked; it needs a **"calculating engine."** I spent that night researching Deterministic Cryptography to build a system where you never have to remember a password again—just a pattern.

😩 The Problems I Was Solving

  • Everyone forgets passwords — and there's no safe way to recover them
  • Password managers store your data in a cloud vault that can be hacked
  • Generated passwords with O1lI0 characters are impossible to type manually
  • "Amazon" vs "Amazon.com" vs "Amazon App" = 3 different passwords — user error nightmare
  • Two accounts on the same platform? Generic generators can't handle it
  • Forced to change password? You'd need a completely new master key
  • One breach of the vault = all your passwords compromised

✅ How FrankPass Solves All of It

  • Remember 1 phrase → get a unique, unbreakable password for every platform
  • Zero database — nothing stored, nothing to hack, ever
  • Smart character set — all ambiguous characters (C, S, V, W, 0, 1) removed by default
  • 28,500+ platform Auto-Suggest normalizes names — type anything, get the same password
  • Username field — two Instagram accounts? Each gets its own unique password
  • Variant counter — change Variant 1→2 to rotate password instantly, no new secret key needed
    • 🔒 Stateless by design — close the tab and every trace is gone. Nothing to breach

With FrankPass, we don't store your passwords. We calculate them. Welcome to true digital freedom.
