1. Privacy Policy (Zero-Data Promise)

You cannot leak what you do not have.

FrankPass is a truly stateless application.

  • No Databases: We do not have a user database. We do not store your name, email, IP address, Master Key, or generated passwords.
  • On-Device Cryptography: All password generation happens locally inside your browser using JavaScript. The mathematical operation executes in your device's RAM and disappears the moment you close the tab or within 15 seconds.
  • No Trackers: We do not use third-party analytics trackers or retargeting pixels on the core generator.
  • Local Storage Only: If you enable Notification Preferences or save basic settings, that data is saved locally on your hardware via HTML5 LocalStorage, not on our cloud.
