1. Privacy Policy — Zero-Data Promise

You cannot leak what you do not have.

FrankPass is a truly stateless application. Our privacy policy is simple because our data collection is zero.

  • No Databases: We do not maintain a user database. We do not store your name, email address, IP address, Secret Key, or any generated passwords — because we never receive them.
  • On-Device Cryptography: All password generation happens locally inside your browser using the Web Cryptography API. The mathematical operation executes in your device's RAM and is cleared within 15 seconds of generation, or when you close the tab.
  • No Trackers: We do not use third-party analytics trackers, retargeting pixels, or advertising networks on the core generator.
  • Local Storage Only: If you choose to save settings (e.g., platform preferences), that data is saved locally on your device via browser storage — not on our servers. You control it, and you can clear it at any time through your browser settings.
  • Payment Data: Pro plan payments are processed by our third-party payment processor (Dodo Payments). FrankPass never sees or stores your card details. Refer to Dodo's privacy policy for their data handling practices.

6. Security Architecture & Scope

What FrankPass Protects — and What It Doesn't

What FrankPass secures: The password generation and storage layer. By storing nothing, we eliminate the most common attack vector — server-side breaches. FrankPass uses PBKDF2 with HMAC-SHA512 at 1,000,000 iterations, running exclusively via the browser-native Web Cryptography API. These are NIST-approved, industry-standard cryptographic primitives. No custom or proprietary cryptography is used.

What FrankPass cannot protect against: Keyloggers or malware on your local device; phishing attacks where you voluntarily enter your Secret Key on a fake website; shoulder surfing; a weak or guessable Secret Key; browser extensions with malicious permissions; or any attack that occurs after you copy and paste your generated password.

Responsible Disclosure: If you discover a security vulnerability in FrankPass, please report it responsibly via direct message to @iamfrankpass on X before making any public disclosure. We take security reports seriously and will respond promptly.
